Our Roots: A Legacy in Bloom

Samparya Bloom

For over three decades, Samparyá Bloom has remained a beloved symbol of timeless beauty and craftsmanship in the Philippines. Established with a passion for elegance and an eye for detail, our flower shop has grown from a humble local boutique into a trusted destination for those who appreciate premium floral artistry.

Each arrangement we create is more than just a bouquet—it's a story of grace, care, and connection. Every stem is thoughtfully chosen, and every design is crafted to bring warmth and sophistication to life’s most meaningful moments.

Our floral creations are inspired by the language of flowers—capturing fleeting emotions and transforming them into lasting memories. Whether it’s a gesture of love, celebration, or comfort, we design each piece to express what words sometimes cannot.

Our commitment to excellence goes beyond beautiful blooms. Whether you're ordering online for same-day delivery in select cities or visiting one of our boutiques in person, we ensure that every experience with Samparyá Bloom feels personal, seamless, and inspiring.

From our friendly florists to our refined presentation, every step of our process is guided by a deep understanding of how flowers can transform ordinary spaces into extraordinary expressions of emotion. We believe that details matter, and it’s these subtle touches that elevate our creations.

Through the years, we’ve had the honor of designing floral arrangements for homes, weddings, corporate events, and meaningful celebrations of life. From adding charm to intimate spaces to elevating grand venues, Samparyá Bloom continues to capture the essence of every story we touch—one petal at a time.

PURPOSE

Our story

Founded in Angeles City, Pampanga, Samparya Bloom has been dedicated to bringing people together through the beauty of flowers for near 30 years. The name "Samparya" comes from the Kapampangan word meaning "to gather" or "to bring together," perfectly reflecting our mission to create bonds, celebrate moments, and share joy through stunning floral artistry.

What began as a small family-run flower shop has grown into a well-loved brand known for premium floral arrangements and decor. Over the years, we've expanded from crafting lovely bouquets for intimate occasions to styling large-scale events and transforming spaces with our unique designs.

In 2008, we launched our online store to make our flowers accessible to everyone, offering same-day delivery and a seamless way to send love and beauty straight to someone's doorstep.

"Every flower tells a story. It's our privilege to help you tell yours."

Meet The Founder

Casey Ong
Built a Legacy in Bloom

Founder & Creative Director

Casey Ong founded Samparyá Bloom in 1995 with a simple vision: to bring people together through the universal language of flowers. What started as a small flower cart in Angeles City has blossomed into one of the Philippines' most beloved floral brands.

With over 30 years of expertise in floral design, Casey has transformed countless spaces and touched countless hearts. Her passion for capturing emotion through petals and stems has earned Samparyá Bloom recognition across the nation.

"I believe every arrangement should be more than beautiful—it should be meaningful," Casey shares. "Whether it's a simple bouquet or an elaborate wedding installation, our goal is always the same: to create moments that last forever."
