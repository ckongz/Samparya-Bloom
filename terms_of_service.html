Terms of Service

By placing an order through our website, you are agreeing to the terms and conditions outlined below.

ORDERS

By placing an order, you reserve the right to us decline the inclusion of non-Samparya Bloom items or deliveries (e.g., gifts, toys, chocolates, or handwritten cards). We are not liable for the loss of, fines or delays due to such items.

Please note that some cakes cannot be customized due to our agreement with suppliers and bakeries or pre-set locations.

A processed message can be added to your arrangement, which will be printed on our signature Samparya Bloom card.

CANCELLATION POLICY

Orders may be cancelled or changed at least 24 hours before the scheduled delivery date to receive a full refund. Cancellations made within 24 hours (on the delivery date) will be charged in full.

To request a cancellation or change, please email us at service@samparyabloom.ph or call our customer support team.

RETURNS AND REFUNDS

Due to the perishable nature of our products, we are unable to offer returns within 24 hours of delivery.

At Samparya Bloom, every arrangement is a special expression of love and care. If your order arrives in a condition that does not meet our quality standards, please contact our support team at service@samparyabloom.ph within 24 hours of delivery. Please include the following:

  • Proof of purchase (order number, order form, or receipt)
  • Clear photos of the arrangement

PRODUCTS AND SUBSTITUTION POLICY

All products are subject to seasonal availability.

While we strive to deliver exactly what is shown on our website, substitutions may occur due to product availability. This could include changes in flower types, color, or packaging.

Rest assured, any substitutions will be of equal or greater value, ensuring complete customer satisfaction. Please note: No changes can be made to your order once received.

PAYMENTS

We accept payments through credit card, debit card, and over the counter (OTC) banking.

All transactions are processed securely through Maya and PayMongo payment gateways.

For over-the-counter payments, orders will be confirmed once proof of payment has been received via email or through our website.

Any instances of credit card fraud will be reported to the appropriate authorities.
